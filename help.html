Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions our clients ask.

What documents are required for DSC registration?
Generally, you need a copy of your PAN card, Aadhaar card, a passport-sized photograph, and a mobile number linked to Aadhaar for OTP verification. For Class 3 DSC, video verification may also be required.
How long does it take to file a tender?
With TenderX, most tenders can be processed within 15 minute once all documents are provided. Urgent filings can be prioritized for same-day submission.
Which portals do you support for tender filing?
We support all major government portals including CPPP (Central Public Procurement Portal), GeM (Government e-Marketplace), JK Tenders, and other state-specific e-procurement portals.
Is my data secure with TenderX?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption protocols and secure cloud storage to ensure your business documents and digital signatures are never compromised.
Can I track the status of my tender application?
Yes, if you have an account, you can log in to your dashboard to see real-time updates on your submission, from document review to final portal submission.
How can I register for GST in Ramban?
You can visit our office near the Ramban Bus Stand with your PAN, Aadhaar, and business address proof. We handle the entire registration process online for J&K businesses.
What are the deadlines for GST return filing in J&K?
Deadlines vary (usually 11th or 20th of every month) depending on your turnover and scheme. We provide monthly reminders to all our Ramban clients to avoid late fees.
